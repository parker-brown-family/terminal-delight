# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want terminal-delight to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. Contributors and maintainers pledge to make
participation a respectful and constructive experience for all.

## Our standards

- Be kind, patient, and welcoming. Assume good faith.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Respect differing viewpoints, experience levels, and time zones.
- Focus on what's best for the project and its users.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, personal or political attacks,
publishing others' private information without consent, and any conduct that a
reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting. This
project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as the authoritative, detailed standard; please read it for the full list of
expected and prohibited behaviours.

## Scope

This Code applies in all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests,
discussions — and whenever someone is representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to **tools@intellimass.ai**. All reports will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the reporter's privacy and
safety will be respected. Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate,
up to and including a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces, and will
follow the Contributor Covenant's
[Enforcement Guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines)
when deciding consequences.
